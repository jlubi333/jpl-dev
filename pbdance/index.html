Welcome to Palm Beach Dancing! Whether you aim to dance for fun or for sport, we can help you in your journey. We can help you look great on the dance floor—and off. Dancing is great conditioning for the mind and body, so why don't you give us a call at 1‑561‑932‑6660? There's nothing to lose.

During your one-on-one private lessons, you will receive personalized instruction that will help you achieve your goals. We offer private instruction for singles and couples of all ages and abilities. We schedule lessons at your convience—ideal if you're a busy professional! Your instructor will spend the necessary time on technique, styling, and anything else that you could need.
