JPL Platformer

An eternal night has befallen the peaceful lands of Lubinia. Enemies and other dangers are abound, not to mention the horrid Preda. You will not want to stick around Lubinia for more than two minutes if you value your life. No Square has seen the mythical Preda and lived to tell the story. If you do manage to defeat the Preda, how long can you survive The Lag? There is only one way to find out. You must work together with your friends to save the day—but, watch out—your friends may soon become your enemies. Only one can vanquish the eternal night; will it be you?
